Fish Specimen Datasets

The list of fish datasets in the world.

  1. Fishnet.AI

    Comments: There are few high-quality images in this dataset. First, not all images have fish. We need an ML algorithm to detect if there is a fish in the picture. Furthermore, most images are blurry due to poor visibility and low resolution, this results in a jagged outline of the fish in the picture, and the scales cannot be seen clearly. In addition, humans, knives, bloodstains, or other objects obstruct the fish, so some of them have missing parts. Besides, the sides of the fish photographed are very different. For those images of relatively good quality, we can roughly see the shape and position of the fins, the fish eyes, the outline of the fish, and the skin color. Perhaps this dataset could be used as a test set for our ML works.
